About Us

Empowering Investors in the Unlisted Space

Welcome to Unlisted Equities, your trusted platform for trading unlisted shares and pre-IPO stocks of India’s most promising private companies. We’re on a mission to democratize access to opportunities that were once reserved for a select few.

  • Investor data and interest is handled securely and confidentially.
